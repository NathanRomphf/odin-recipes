Spaghetti

Description

Our favorite spaghetti is kid-friendly and made with a delicious homemade sauce making it the perfect family dinner recipe!

This spaghetti is perfect if your lazy and need to make dinner without putting in any of the effort, but then want to yell at your significant other for how little they do!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Jar of Tomato Sauce
  • Boxed Spaghetti
  • Garlic
  • Mushrooms
  • Carrots

Steps

  1. Brown the hamburger in a pan
  2. Cook the sauce on low heat adding whatever you want to it
  3. Cook the pasta according to the box (hopefully you can follow instructions)
  4. Combine and enjoy, or don't. I really don't care
