Deluxe Pizza

Description

The absolute most delicious pizza you will ever make for your family.

This pizza is so good it may even make your kids love you again.

Ingredients

  • Sliced Green Peppers
  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Mushrooms
  • Pepperoni
